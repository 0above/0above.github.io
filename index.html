About Our Distro

Welcome to WhiteOar, your go-to ethical hacking distribution built on Debian. We've designed it specifically to run smoothly on low-end systems without compromising performance. With WhiteOar, you get a robust set of hacking tools that are ready to use right out of the box, all configured for maximum efficiency. WhiteOar isn't just about functionality; it's also about style. Our clean, modern design ensures everything is organized intuitively, and we provide regular updates to keep your experience fresh and productive.

Whether you're a seasoned cybersecurity professional, a penetration tester, a digital forensics analyst, or just someone passionate about security, WhiteOar is for you. Its lightweight nature makes it perfect for those with limited hardware, and its fast, responsive environment is ideal for anyone looking to streamline their work. Plus, our comprehensive toolset and easy-to-navigate interface make it great for both pros and beginners alike, offering a perfect platform for learning, experimenting, and mastering cybersecurity practices.

Tools&Applications

LAZYPARIAH

LAZYPARIAH is a simple and easily installable command-line tool written in pure Ruby that can generate a range of reverse shell payloads on the fly.

DNSWatch

DNSWatch is a powerful packet sniffing tool designed to monitor and analyze DNS (Domain Name System) traffic on a network.

Nikto

Nikto is an Open Source (GPL) web server scanner which performs comprehensive tests against web servers for multiple items.

BeEF

BeEF is short for The Browser Exploitation Framework. It is a penetration testing tool that focuses on the web browser.

RustScan

RustScan is a modern take on the port scanner. Sleek&fast. All while providing extensive extendability to you.

Fluxion

Fluxion is a security auditing and social-engineering research tool. It is a remake of linset by vk496 with fewer bugs and more functionality.
